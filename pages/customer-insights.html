Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the sentiment analysis? +
We use GPT-4 which achieves 85-92% accuracy on sentiment classification. We also train the model on your specific industry language during setup to improve accuracy for your context.
What review sites can you integrate? +
Google Reviews, Yelp, Facebook, Trustpilot, G2, Capterra, and most industry-specific review platforms. We'll confirm during the demo which sources are compatible.
Can it analyze past feedback or just new feedback? +
Both. We import and analyze your historical data (typically 6-12 months back) so you can see trends immediately. Then it automatically processes all new feedback daily.
What if we don't have many reviews yet? +
You need at least 50-100 feedback items for meaningful analysis. If you're below that threshold, we can help you set up automated feedback collection first (surveys, post-purchase emails, etc.).
Is customer data kept private? +
Yes. All analysis happens through encrypted connections. Customer names and personal info are anonymized in the dashboard. We can sign an NDA and BAA if needed.
Can I customize what metrics I see? +
Absolutely. During setup, we configure the dashboard around your specific business metrics and priorities. Want to track mentions of specific features? Competitor comparisons? Custom categories? We build it your way.
What format are the insights delivered in? +
You get a web dashboard you can check anytime, plus automated email reports (daily urgent alerts, weekly summaries, monthly trends). Export to Excel/PDF anytime.
