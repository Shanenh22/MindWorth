Frequently Asked Questions

Will it sound like generic AI content? +
Not when done right. We train the AI on YOUR existing content, your voice, your industry language. The output sounds like you wrote it (just faster). You always edit before publishing, so you maintain authenticity and quality control.
Do I still need to edit the AI output? +
Yes, but minimally. Think of AI as giving you a 70-80% complete first draft. You review for accuracy, add personal touches, adjust tone, and approve. This takes 5-10 minutes vs. 1-2 hours of starting from scratch.
What AI tools do you use? +
Primarily GPT-4 through ChatGPT Plus or API, with custom prompts and templates we build for you. We also integrate with tools like Jasper, Copy.ai, or custom solutions depending on your needs and budget.
Can it write long-form content like blog posts? +
Yes. We typically generate outlines, then expand section by section. For a 1,500-word blog post, AI generates the first draft in 10-15 minutes. You spend 30-45 minutes editing and refining vs. 3-4 hours writing from scratch.
Is AI-generated content bad for SEO? +
No. Google doesn't penalize AI content‚Äîthey penalize low-quality content. If the content is accurate, helpful, and edited by humans, it performs well. We train the AI to include SEO best practices (keywords, structure, readability).
What if I have a very specific niche? +
That's actually ideal. During setup, we feed the AI your industry terminology, competitor content, customer language, and your best-performing pieces. The more specific your niche, the better we can train the system.
Can it generate visual content too (images, graphics)? +
We focus on text content (the bottleneck for most businesses). However, we can integrate with tools like Canva or DALL-E for image generation as an add-on. Most clients pair our text automation with simple Canva templates.
