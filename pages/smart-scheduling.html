Frequently Asked Questions

Which scheduling tools do you use? +
We typically use Calendly, Acuity Scheduling, or Cal.com depending on your needs. All integrate with Google Calendar, Outlook, and most CRMs. We'll recommend the best fit during your assessment.
Can customers pay when they book? +
Yes. We can integrate Stripe or Square for deposits, full payments, or cancellation fees. This significantly reduces no-shows (people are less likely to miss paid appointments).
What if I need to schedule multiple staff members? +
No problem. We can set up team scheduling with round-robin distribution, priority-based assignment, or customer choice. Each team member's calendar syncs individually.
How do SMS reminders work? +
We integrate with Twilio to send automated text reminders. You pay per message sent (typically $0.01-0.03 each). SMS reminders reduce no-shows by an additional 20-30% compared to email alone.
Can I block out personal time or vacations? +
Absolutely. You control your availability settings. Block out lunch breaks, personal appointments, PTO‚Äîanything on your calendar automatically becomes unavailable for booking.
What if I need different service durations? +
Easy. We set up different appointment types (e.g., "30-min consultation," "1-hour session," "2-hour deep clean"). Customers select the service when booking, and the system reserves the correct time block.
Can I still manually add appointments? +
Yes. You retain full control. You can manually add, edit, or cancel appointments anytime. The system just prevents double-bookings and handles automated communications.
