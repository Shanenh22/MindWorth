Example Follow-Up Sequence

What a typical nurture campaign looks like

Day 0 (Immediate)

Subject: Thanks for your interest, [Name]
Confirm we received their inquiry. Provide helpful resource relevant to their interest.

Day 2

Subject: How [Similar Company] solved [Their Problem]
Share a relevant case study. No hard sell, just value.

Day 5

Subject: Quick question about [Their Goal]
Ask about their timeline and specific needs. Open conversation.

Day 9

Subject: [Video] See how it works in 90 seconds
Short demo video or product walkthrough. Make it easy to understand.

Day 14

Subject: Limited offer for [Month] only
Special pricing, bonus, or urgency. Clear call-to-action.

Day 21 (Final)

Subject: Should I close your file?
Gentle breakup email. Often triggers response. If no reply, move to cold list.

Note: Sequence pauses automatically if lead replies or books a meeting

Frequently Asked Questions

Won't automated emails feel impersonal? +
Not when done right. We personalize with names, company info, and behavior triggers. The emails provide value (case studies, insights, resources)‚Äînot generic spam. Most recipients can't tell they're automated.
Which CRM do you integrate with? +
HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho, Close, Monday.com, and 50+ others. If you don't have a CRM, we'll recommend free options like HubSpot or Zoho for small teams.
What if a lead replies to an automated email? +
The automation pauses immediately and notifies you. You take over with a personal response. The system is smart enough to know when human touch is needed.
Can I customize the email sequences? +
Absolutely. We write initial templates, but you can edit anytime. We teach you how to modify timing, add/remove emails, test different messages, and adapt based on results.
How do you avoid spam filters? +
We follow email best practices: proper authentication (SPF, DKIM), avoid spam trigger words, maintain good sender reputation, include unsubscribe links, and use reputable sending platforms.
What's considered a "hot" lead? +
We define scoring rules based on your business. Typically: opened 3+ emails, clicked multiple links, visited pricing page, or engaged with high-value content. You get real-time alerts for these leads.
What happens to leads who don't convert? +
After the main sequence, they move to a "nurture list" receiving monthly valuable content. Some leads need 6-12 months. The system keeps them warm until they're ready to buy.
