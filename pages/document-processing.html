Frequently Asked Questions

What if my documents have unique formats? +
That's normal. We train the AI specifically on YOUR document formats during setup. Bring us your messiest invoices‚Äîwe'll make it work. The more variety you show us, the better we can train the system.
Can it handle handwritten documents? +
Yes, but with limitations. Clean handwriting works well (80-90% accuracy). Messy handwriting is harder (60-70%). We recommend typed/printed documents when possible, but we can process handwritten forms with a human review step.
How do I get documents into the system? +
Multiple options: (1) Email PDFs to a dedicated address, (2) Upload to Dropbox/Google Drive folder, (3) Scan using mobile app, (4) Forward from your email. We set up whatever works best for your workflow.
What happens when extraction is uncertain? +
The system flags low-confidence extractions for human review. You see the document with highlighted uncertain fields, verify/correct quickly, and approve. This takes seconds vs. minutes of full manual entry.
Which accounting software can you integrate with? +
QuickBooks (Online & Desktop), Xero, FreshBooks, Wave, Sage, and most major platforms. We can also push data to Excel, Google Sheets, Airtable, or custom databases. If it has an API, we can connect.
How long does processing take? +
Typically 10-30 seconds per document for extraction and data push. For a batch of 50 invoices, total processing time is under 5 minutes. Compare that to 6-8 hours of manual entry.
Is my document data secure? +
Yes. We use enterprise-grade OCR services (Google Cloud, AWS) with encryption in transit and at rest. Documents are processed and deleted (not stored long-term unless you want backups). We can sign NDAs and BAAs.
